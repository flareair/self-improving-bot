# Remote Work & Home Office Equipment

## Remote Work Eligibility

Employees in fully remote or hybrid roles (per their offer letter) may work from any location approved by their manager and within a country where the company has an established legal entity. Hybrid employees must be on-site on their team's designated in-office days.

## Home Office Equipment Stipend

New remote and hybrid employees receive a one-time $500 home office stipend for desk, chair, monitor, and similar equipment, expensable through the standard Expense Portal within the first 90 days of starting. After the initial stipend, additional equipment requests go through IT rather than the Expense Portal.

## Equipment Approval Process

Any individual equipment purchase or request over $200 — whether from the stipend or a later ad hoc request — requires manager sign-off before purchase. IT provisions and images all laptops directly; employees do not purchase their own laptops. Peripherals and accessories under $200 can be requested directly through the IT Portal without additional approval.

# Benefits & Payroll

## Health Insurance Enrollment

New employees have 30 days from their start date to enroll in a health plan; coverage is retroactive to day one. Outside of that window, changes are only allowed during the annual open enrollment period or after a qualifying life event (marriage, birth, loss of other coverage).

## Wellness Stipend

Employees receive a $50/month wellness stipend for gym memberships, fitness classes, or wellness apps. Submit receipts through the Expense Portal within **30 days** of the charge to be reimbursed; the stipend does not roll over month to month.

## Payroll Schedule

Employees are paid on the 15th and last business day of each month. Direct deposit changes must be submitted at least 5 business days before a pay date to take effect for that cycle.

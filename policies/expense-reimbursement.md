# Expense Reimbursement

## Submitting an Expense Report

Submit expenses through the Expense Portal with an itemized receipt attached to each line item. Expenses without a receipt over $25 will not be reimbursed except in documented cases where a receipt genuinely could not be obtained. Your manager approves expenses under $500; anything at or above $500 also requires Finance sign-off.

## Reimbursable Travel Expenses

Reasonable costs for approved business travel are reimbursable: economy-class flights, standard hotel rooms, ground transportation, and meals up to the daily per-diem rate for the destination city (see the Travel Portal for current per-diem rates by location). Upgrades, personal entertainment, and travel for a companion are not reimbursable.

## Client Gifts & Entertainment

Client meals and entertainment up to $150 per person are reimbursable with the client's name and business purpose noted on the expense line. Gifts to a client or vendor over $75 in value require prior written approval from your manager — see the Security & Code of Conduct policy for the underlying conflict-of-interest rules this exists to support.

## Reimbursement Deadline

Expense reports must be submitted within **45 days** of the date the expense was incurred. Reports submitted after 45 days will be denied except in extenuating circumstances approved by Finance.
